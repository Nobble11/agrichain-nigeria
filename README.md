# AgriChain-Nigeria
AgriChain Nigeria is an open-source Solana-based platform that enables transparent farm record-keeping and community-powered financing for smallholder farmers.

Problem

Millions of smallholder farmers in Nigeria struggle to access affordable financing and maintain trustworthy records of their farming activities. Existing systems are fragmented, paper-based, and difficult for investors, cooperatives, and lenders to verify. This lack of transparency limits access to capital and reduces trust between farmers and funding providers.

Solution

AgriChain Nigeria is an open-source prototype built on Solana that enables farmers to create transparent, verifiable records of farming activities while allowing communities and supporters to fund agricultural projects transparently.
The platform will record key farming milestones such as land preparation, planting, crop growth, harvest, and produce sales. These records will be stored and timestamped on Solana, creating a transparent history that can be independently verified.
In addition, the prototype will demonstrate a community funding mechanism where supporters can contribute funds to agricultural projects and track project progress through on-chain updates.

Why Solana? 

Solana provides fast transaction speeds, low fees, and transparent record keeping, making it suitable for agricultural record management and community funding applications.
